Indus Valley Civilization Part-II

Hrappa :-

    Archaeological Resources :-
  • 6 granaries in row
  • Working floors Stone symbols of Lingam (Male sex organ), Virgin-Goddess (Seal), Stone symbols of Lingam (Male sex organ), Yoni (Female sex organ)
  • Clay figure of Mother Goddess
  • Wheat and Barley in wooden morter, Copper Scale, Copper-made mirron, Vanity box, Dice

Mahenjodaro (UNESCO World Heritage Site) :-

  • The largest in Indus Civilization.
  • Rakhigiri the largest site of Indus Civilization in India.
    • Archaeological Resources :-
  • Great granaries, Great Bath (The largest building of civilization).
  • Pashupati Mahadeva / Proto-Shiva seal.
  • Bronze Image of Nude women dancer , Image of bearded man.
  • Clay figure of Mother Goddess.
  • Mesopotemian seals (50% of total seals of civilization), cotton, Brick Kilns, Dice.

Chanhudaro :-

  • City without a Citadel
    • Archaeological Resources :-
  • Inkpot, Lipstick, Imprint of dogs's paw in bricks,
  • Terracotta model of a bullock cart
  • Clay figure of Mother Goddess
  • Mesopotemian seals (50% of total seals of civilization), cotton, Brick Kilns, Dice

Lothal :-

  • Dockyard ( Lothal was a port city)
    • Archaeological Resources :-
  • Fire alerts,
  • Terracotta model of a horse
  • Double burial (burying male and female in a single grave)
  • Mesopotemian seals (50% of total seals of civilization), cotton, Brick Kilns, Dice

Kalibanga :-

    Archaeological Resources :-
  • 7 Fire alerts,
  • Mesopotemian seals, decorated bricks

Banawali :-

    Archaeological Resources :-
  • Lack of chess board, Lack of drainage systen

Dholavira :-

    Archaeological Resources :-
  • A unique water harnessing system and its storm water drainage system
  • A large well and a bath (largest water reservoirs )
  • only city divided into 3 parts
  • a largest Harappan inscription, a stadium

Surkotada :-

  • The only site where remains of horses found
    • Archaeological Resources :-
  • Bone of horses

Daimabad :-

    Archaeological Resources :-
  • Bronze images (Chariot, ox, elephant, rhinoeros)
